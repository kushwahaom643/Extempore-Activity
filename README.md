## Topic Name

Design Thinking Practicing for Co-Curricular Skills

## Task Number

Task #08 – Extempore Activity

## Objective

To develop communication, critical thinking, and presentation skills by preparing extempore speeches on technology-oriented topics.

## Topics Covered

### 1. IT in Automobile

This extempore discusses the role of Information Technology in modern automobiles, including smart vehicles, autonomous driving, embedded systems, GPS navigation, predictive maintenance, and advanced safety features.

### 2. IT in Metro Rail

This extempore highlights the importance of Information Technology in metro rail systems, including automated ticketing, train scheduling, passenger information systems, surveillance, and smart transportation management.

### 3. IT in Avionics

This extempore explains the application of Information Technology in aviation, including navigation systems, communication systems, flight control, weather monitoring, air traffic management, and aircraft safety.
